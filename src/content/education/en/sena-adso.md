## About the institution

The Servicio Nacional de Aprendizaje (SENA) is a Colombian public body under the Ministry of Labor that offers free professional training to millions of Colombians every year. It's one of the country's main references for technical and technological education, with a presence in every region and ties with the industry.

## About the program

The Software Analysis and Development Technologist program (ADSO) is a technological degree oriented to training professionals capable of analyzing, designing, building, deploying and maintaining software solutions following modern life cycles and industry standards.

### 01 · Systems analysis and design

Requirements modeling, UML, layered architecture, design patterns and technical documentation.

### 02 · Programming and databases

Object-oriented programming languages, data structures, SQL, relational modeling and NoSQL databases.

### 03 · Software engineering

Agile methodologies (Scrum/Kanban), version control, testing, continuous integration and deployment.

## Duration and format

- Total duration: 3,984 hours between lecture stage and productive stage.
- Mixed format: in-person / virtual classes depending on the training center.
- Mandatory productive stage at the end of the program (internship or apprenticeship contract).
