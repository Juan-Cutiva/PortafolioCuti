## What I learned

- Logical thinking and problem solving applied to programming.
- Semantic HTML: landmarks, headings, forms and their impact on accessibility and SEO.
- Modern CSS: box model, Flexbox, CSS Grid and responsive design with media queries.
- First steps with JavaScript: variables, types, control flow and basic DOM manipulation.
- End-to-end frontend workflow: editor, browser, dev tools and deployment.
