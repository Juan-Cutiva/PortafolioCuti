I was born in **Colombia** and I live in Bogotá. My relationship with tech has been there pretty much forever: I grew up in an environment where fixing computers, talking about servers or getting hands-on with the technical side was just normal. That's where the interest came from… and with time, a love for programming.

What started as curiosity —a simple tutorial— ended up turning into hours in front of the computer building interfaces and understanding how things actually work under the hood.

I've been learning on my own for a couple of years now, and I'm also studying the **Software Analysis and Development Technologist** program at **SENA**. I like that mix: having solid foundations, but also learning by doing, trying things and messing up.

Outside the technical side, I'm someone calm, observant and pretty stubborn when something really matters to me. I prefer to understand things well before acting, and when something hooks me, I go all the way in.
